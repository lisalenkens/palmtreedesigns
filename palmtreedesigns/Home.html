PalmTreeDesigns

PalmTreeDesigns was founded in 2018 by Lisa Lenkens. PalmTreeDesigns is inspired by the beauty of nature, in particular Palm Trees. The leaves are majestic and sharp, but at the same time welcoming and humble and I think they are just a beautiful piece of nature.. I associate them with sun, nature, tropical vibes, relaxness and positive feelings. When you wear a PalmTreeDesigns shirt, I want you to feel comfortable, positive, unique and confident! You're beautiful inside out, and with a unique design you created yourself (with a little help from me) you can show people who you are!
